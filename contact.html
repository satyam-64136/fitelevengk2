FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Is the free trial really free? +

Yes — completely. No card, no contract, no hidden charges. Just walk in, try any class or use the facility, and see if Fit Eleven is the right fit for you. We're confident you'll love it.

What membership plans are available? +

We offer monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and annual memberships. Special women's day offers (25% off), referral discounts, and corporate rates are also available. Contact us for the latest pricing.

Are the defence / boxing classes beginner-friendly? +

Absolutely. Our combat coaches train all levels — from complete beginners to experienced athletes. The defence class is designed specifically for everyday people learning real self-protection techniques, not just fighters.

Do you offer personal training for women? +

Yes — our personal trainers work with members of all genders. We have dedicated female trainers and a strong women's community at Fit Eleven. Many of our most inspiring transformations are from our women members.

Where exactly is the gym located? +

We're on the 2nd and 3rd floor of M Block Market, Greater Kailash II, New Delhi — 110048. Easily accessible via metro (Greater Kailash station) and by car with parking nearby. Get directions →
